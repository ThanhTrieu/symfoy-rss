  • Audi Sportscar Experience - Audi RS5 track drive

    The Audi A5 family gained a new member in April this year – the Audi RS5. The most powerful and the most expensive comrade of the ‘Brat Pack’, as Audi likes to call it, was among the cars present at the 7th edition of the Audi Sportscar Experience. So I jumped into the driver’s seat of one for a few laps of the Buddh International Circuit to find out if a 450 PS 2.9 L V6 with AWD in a gorgeous 2-door coupe is as delicious as it sounds. The stock Pirelli tyres are replaced with sleek Metzeler rubber, while there are custom exhaust pipes in place of the twin canisters. Old Empire Motorcycles
