Audi Sportscar Experience - Audi RS5 track drive

Aug 20, 2018 - 6:36pm IST | Audi A5, Car Reviews, Audi | Bruce Nguyen
Overview

The Audi A5 family gained a new member in April this year – the Audi RS5. The most powerful and the most expensive comrade of the ‘Brat Pack’, as Audi likes to call it, was among the cars present at the 7th edition of the Audi Sportscar Experience. So I jumped into the driver’s seat of one for a few laps of the Buddh International Circuit to find out if a 450 PS 2.9 L V6 with AWD in a gorgeous 2-door coupe is as delicious as it sounds.
Old Empire Motorcycles have opted for a minimalist design with custom handlebars and a chopped tail section with a leatherette tan, brown quilted seat. The bike builder has done away with the front and rear fenders, indicators, taillight and the headlamp as well.

The stock Pirelli tyres are replaced with sleek Metzeler rubber, while there are custom exhaust pipes in place of the twin canisters. Old Empire Motorcycles have neatly routed the accelerator and clutch cables through the handlebars. The custom Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 gets matte black treatment to the engine and wheels with golden highlights.

Interestingly though, the custom builder has done away with the clutch and front brake levers altogether. The custom Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 receives a deep metallic red paint scheme with Oxblood leather and Alcantara seat upholstery. The conventional keyhole has been replaced with a keyless ignition system
